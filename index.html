Professional Communications - Short Course

Online Short Course for all Professionals

This is a 4-topic short course aimed at profossionals who want to expand and improve their communications skills in the modern workplace environment.

Students can access the content, download the files, and complete regular tasks for each topic. Master the subtle art of workplace communication.

Topic 1 - Introduction

An introduction to the core principles that underpin modern professional communications. Students will examine the psychology of communication, expression, and the process of sharing understanding.

Topic 2 - Business Language

This topic deals with the importance of choosing our words carefully. Students look at the nuances of language, its strengths and weaknesses, and how to build a clear written message.

Topic 3 - Digital Communications

A deeper look into how the digital medium has changed modern workplace communications. This topic will help students navigate the traps, and leverage the potential of all forms of digital communications.

Topic 4 - Proposal Documents

A written proposal is the kind of document that can dramatically change an organisation. This topic helps students to understand the key ingredients of documents intended to convince their audience.

A Range of Free Courses

We offer a variety of short courses aimed at the busy modern professional.

Each course is short and to the point. We focus on practical knowledge and useful theory.
